# 3. User Personas

← [Previous](02_Problem_Statement.md) | [Next](04_Solution_Overview.md)

## Persona 1: Full-Time Delivery Partner

- Location: Chennai  
- Platform: Zepto  
- Work Type: Full-time  

### Profile
Works 8–10 hours daily and depends entirely on delivery income.

### Pain Points
- income drops during rain and heat  
- no backup financial support  
- high dependency on daily earnings  

---

## Persona 2: Part-Time Student Worker

- Location: Bangalore  
- Platform: Blinkit  
- Work Type: Part-time  

### Profile
Works evenings to support expenses.

### Pain Points
- limited working hours  
- disruptions reduce already low earnings  

---

## Persona 3: Multi-Platform Worker

- Location: Mumbai  
- Platforms: Zepto + Swiggy  

### Profile
Works across platforms to maximize income.

### Pain Points
- disruptions affect all platforms simultaneously  
- inconsistent earnings  

---

## Persona 4: Night Shift Worker

- Location: Delhi  

### Profile
Works late-night hours where conditions are unpredictable.

### Pain Points
- safety concerns  
- low demand during disruptions  

---

## Persona 5: New Delivery Partner

- Location: Hyderabad  

### Profile
Recently joined, still stabilizing income.

### Pain Points
- no financial buffer  
- highly vulnerable to disruptions
