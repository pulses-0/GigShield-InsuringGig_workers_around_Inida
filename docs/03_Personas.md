## Primary Persona
- Full-Time Quick Commerce Delivery Partner

# Profile

- Full-time delivery worker (e.g., Zepto / Blinkit)

- Works 8–12 hours daily

- Operates in a fixed delivery zone (2–5 km radius)

- Depends entirely on gig income

# Income Characteristics

- Earns per delivery (no fixed salary)

- Weekly payout cycle

- Income highly variable

- Strong dependency on continuous availability

# Pain Points

- Income stops immediately during disruptions

- No compensation for:

- - heavy rain

- - extreme heat

- - pollution

- - strikes / curfews

- - Platform ratings drop due to delays (even if not their fault)

- - No financial safety net

# Triggers That Affect Them

- Heavy rainfall / flooding

- Extreme heat (>45°C)

- AQI spikes (>400)

- Traffic congestion

- Fuel shortages

- Platform outages

# Needs

- Instant financial protection

- No manual claim process

- Fast payouts (same-day)

- Simple and affordable pricing

# Goals

- Maintain stable weekly income

- Reduce risk from external disruptions

- Avoid income loss during downtime

# How GigShield Helps

- Automatically detects disruptions

- Creates claims without user action

- Validates using AI + data

- Sends instant payouts
