# 1. Introduction

[Next](02_Problem_Statement.md)->

GigShield is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to provide income protection for Q-Commerce delivery partners.

Quick Commerce (Q-Commerce) platforms such as Blinkit and Zepto operate on ultra-fast delivery models where workers complete deliveries within 10–30 minutes. These workers depend heavily on continuous availability to maintain their income.

However, their earnings are highly vulnerable to external disruptions such as:

- extreme weather conditions (rain, heat)
- air pollution
- traffic congestion
- fuel shortages
- strikes and curfews

Even short interruptions during peak hours can lead to significant income loss.

GigShield addresses this problem by introducing a parametric insurance model where payouts are triggered automatically based on real-world conditions, without requiring manual claims or documentation.

The platform combines:

- real-time data monitoring
- AI-driven risk assessment
- automated claims processing
- instant payout mechanisms

to create a seamless and reliable income protection system tailored for gig workers.
