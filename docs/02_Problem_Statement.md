# 2. Problem Statement

← [Previous](01_Introduction.md) | [Next](03_Personas.md)

Q-Commerce delivery partners operate in a highly time-sensitive and performance-driven environment.

Their income is directly proportional to the number of deliveries completed. Any disruption in their ability to work results in immediate income loss.

## Key Challenges

- No work = No income  
- Earnings depend on continuous availability  
- High exposure to environmental and urban disruptions  
- No existing insurance for temporary income loss  

## Real-World Impact

A delivery partner earning approximately ₹200 per hour can lose:

- ₹400–₹800 during a 2–4 hour disruption  
- up to 20–30% of monthly income  

## Current Gap

Existing insurance solutions:
- focus on health, accidents, or assets  
- require manual claims  
- involve long processing times  

There is currently no solution that provides:

- real-time protection  
- automated claim processing  
- coverage for temporary income disruption  

GigShield aims to fill this gap.
