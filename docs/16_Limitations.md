# 16. Limitations

← [Previous](15_Non_Functional_Requirements.md) | [Next](17_Innovations.md)

This section outlines the current limitations of the GigShield platform.

---

## 16.1 Data Dependency

- relies heavily on external APIs  
- inaccuracies may affect trigger detection  

---

## 16.2 Mock Integrations

- payment systems are simulated  
- real-world deployment requires full integration  

---

## 16.3 Limited Coverage Scope

- only covers income loss due to disruptions  
- excludes health, accidents, and vehicle damage  

---

## 16.4 Model Accuracy

- AI models depend on data quality  
- predictions may vary  

---

## 16.5 Geographic Constraints

- system is optimized for specific urban zones  
- requires adaptation for rural areas  

---

## 16.6 Importance

Understanding limitations helps:

- identify improvement areas  
- guide future development  

---
